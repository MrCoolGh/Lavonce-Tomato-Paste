Welcome to Lavonce Tomato Paste.

At Lavonce Tomato Paste., we are dedicated to providing the finest quality tomato paste to households, restaurants, and retailers worldwide. Our products are made from the freshest tomatoes, carefully processed to retain their natural flavor and nutrients.

About Us

Our History

Lavonce Tomato Paste was founded in 2010 with a mission to deliver high-quality tomato products to the global market. Over the years, we have grown into a leading supplier of premium tomato paste, known for our commitment to quality and sustainability.

Our Mission

To provide safe, delicious, and nutritious tomato paste that enhances the culinary experience of our customers.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in tomato paste production by 2030, setting new standards for quality and innovation.

Various meals prepared with the product

Tomato Stew

Our classic tomato paste is made from sun-ripened tomatoes, offering a rich and robust flavor. Perfect for soups, stews, and sauces.

Light Soup

Made from organically grown tomatoes, this paste is free from additives and preservatives. Ideal for health-conscious consumers.

Jollof Rice

Add a kick to your dishes with our spicy tomato paste, infused with chili peppers for an extra burst of flavor.

Our Process

At Lavonce Tomato Paste, we follow a meticulous process to ensure the highest quality tomato paste:

Harvesting

We handpick the freshest tomatoes from our farms.

Processing

Tomatoes are washed, sorted, and processed to extract the pulp.

Packaging

The paste is packaged in eco-friendly materials for freshness.
